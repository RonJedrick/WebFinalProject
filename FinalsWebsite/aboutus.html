About Us

I recently started my own business in the Philippines called Literally Escape. We provide readers with a wide selection of books from a variety of genres, ranging from the classics to contemporary bestsellers. We also offer an array of other items such as magazines, stationery, and art supplies. Our main goal is to provide customers with a place to find their perfect escape into the world of literature and creativity. I'm passionate about the idea of making books available to everyone, no matter their budget. That's why I've worked hard to ensure that our prices are very competitive in the market. I'm also proud of the fact that we offer free delivery for orders placed within the Philippines. I hope that with our service, more people can find the joys of reading and discover the power of the written word.
